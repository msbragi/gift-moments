Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to an inclusive experience for everyone

Our Commitment

Gift Moments is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to ensure our content and features are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML and ARIA roles for better screen reader support
  • Keyboard navigable menus and controls
  • High-contrast color schemes for readability
  • Responsive design for all devices and screen sizes
  • Alt text for all meaningful images

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review our website and update features to improve accessibility. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions, please let us know.

Contact

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of Gift Moments, please contact us at contact@packingfuture.com. We value your feedback and will do our best to address your concerns promptly.
