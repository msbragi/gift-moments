Why We Built This

It started with a story. Nana watched her daughter excitedly create a handmade birthday card for her best friend, carefully writing a heartfelt message inside. But the friend's birthday was still weeks away, so they tucked it away "somewhere safe." When the birthday came and went, they frantically searched everywhere but never found the card. Months later, it turned up in an old book—beautiful, heartfelt, and forgotten. Both of them cried. Not because it was too late, but because they realized how many perfect moments we lose to simple forgetfulness.

That's when we knew: the world needs people who remember. Who capture those 2am brilliant ideas. Who turn fleeting inspiration into perfectly timed magic. We're not just building an app—we're helping you become unforgettable to the people you love.

The Moment That Started It All

One evening, Nana was scrolling through old photos and found a video of her friend laughing until she cried. It was taken during a rough period in her friend's life, and that laugh was rare and precious. Nana wanted to send it, but her friend was having a good week—it didn't feel like the right time.

"What if I could save this for when she really needs it?" Nana thought. "What if I could be the friend who always shows up with exactly the right thing at exactly the right moment?"

That's the night Gift Moments was born. From a simple wish to be the friend who never forgets, who always remembers what matters most.
