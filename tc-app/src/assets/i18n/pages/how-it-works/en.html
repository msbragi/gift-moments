How Gift Moments Works

Simple steps to preserve your memories

Step 1: Create Your Capsule

Start by creating a new Gift Moment and give it a meaningful name.

Step 2: Add Your Memories

Upload photos, videos, audio recordings, and write personal messages.

Step 3: Set the Opening Date

Choose when you want your Gift Moment to be opened and shared.

Step 4: Secure and Store

Your memories are safely encrypted and stored until the opening date.
